Tight List:

Face it: if you use headers nested inside of lists, you will get exactly what you deserve.

  • Header1

    Line 1-2 - not a header or paragraph!

  • Header2

    Line 2-2 - not a header or paragraph!

Loose List:

  • Header1

    Line 1-2 - a paragraph

  • Header2

    Line 2-2 - a paragraph

  • Header3

    Line 3-2 - a paragraph
